Who we are

Here at Hyperdrive Solutions, we provide our clients with fast, outstanding IT solutions.
Our team consists of a diverse range of incredible individuals from different backgrounds, both academically and geographically.

An important aspect of our company is our 3 core values.
Respect. Honesty. Teamwork.

These three values are what make Hyperdrive Solutions such an amazing company to be involved with. The respect that we hold for each member of the team, each idea presented and most of all for our clients is what makes every experience with us a positive one.

Honesty is also an integral part of our company. We are always up front with our clients regarding being realistic with the solution requirements, the time frame that it’ll be completed in and with determining fair pricing. Being a small, new start-up company, teamwork is essential.

Our Mission

Our philosophy is about fast and efficient IT solutions for any problems given by our clients.

We pride ourselves on doing what’s best for the client which is why we respect our client’s requests and their ideas, ensuring we meet their specified requirements.

It’s a must that we collaborate effectively to generate creative and innovative solutions for our clients. If you do business with us, we at Hyperdrive Solutions can guarantee that we’ll create the solution you require.
