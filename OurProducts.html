Description of the game

Cyber Champion is a fun and user friendly quiz-game to educate you on how to be cyber secure.
The questions are randomised for each difficulty and never duplicated/repeated for that set until you pass onto the next difficulty.
Whether the answer is right or wrong, you will always be prompted with a simple explanation of the correct answer.

By answering timed questions correctly - you’ll get points!
If you answer them wrong - you'll lose hearts.
This format is fun and engaging as it pushes you to try and get the most points as possible in order to beat your high score!

How to play the game

1. Choose from the two modes that you would like to play =

Difficulty mode

True or False mode



Difficulty mode -

1. Please select a difficulty based on your knowledge of cyber awareness - there is easy, medium or hard.

2. You will be given a question and 4 answers to choose from.

- If you choose the correct answer you'll be given a point and head on to the next question.
- If you choose the wrong answer you will have one more attempt at answering correctly.
- If you get it wrong the second time you will lose a life.
- If you lose all of your lives the game will be over.

3. When there are no more questions left for you to answer or if you have lost all of your lives - you will be prompted with a game over message and your score below it.

4. You then have the option to either retry the questions or return to the main menu.

True or False mode -

1. You will be given a statement and a "true" button or "false" button to choose from.

- If you choose the correct answer, you'll be given a point and will be directed to the next statement.
- If you choose the wrong answer, you'll lose a life.
- If you lose all of your lives, the game will be over.

2. When there are no more questions left for you to answer or if you lost all of your lives, you will be prompted with a message displaying your results out
of 10.

3. You then have the option to either retry the questions or return to the main menu.

Additional Reading for Beginners

For some players who are new to the topic of Cyber Awareness, some of the questions might be quite difficult for beginners of this game.
Therefore, here are a few sample questions and tips in order to make it easier :)

Below is a question you may encounter while playing our game.
If you wanted to answer it correctly, you would choose the last option as it would be the most difficult for someone else to guess.
Once you have chose the correct answer, you would gain a point and move onto the next question.



Another example of an easy question would be - what is the best way to minimise your digital footprint?

A. Take fewer photos with your smartphone
B. Travel less with your smartphone
C. Post less on social media

The answer to this would be - Post less on social media.
This is because, if you don’t post a lot on social media then there won’t be many details of you on social media sites.
Therefore, minimising your digital footprint.

An example of a medium question would be - which ride-sharing service collects the least amount of your data?

A. Uber
B. Lyft
C. Public Taxi Service

The answer to this would be - Public Taxi Service.
This is because, Uber and lyft collect the data that you input into the app, although with a public taxi service, customers usually ring therefore less information is being sent through to the company.

An example of a difficult question would be - a group of computers that is networked together and used by hackers to steal information is called a...?

A. Botnet
B. Rootkit
C. DDoS
D. Operating System

The answer to this would be a Botnet.
